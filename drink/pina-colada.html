Pina Colada

Intro

Its a pina colada, it doesn't need an introduction dummy.

Ingredients

  • Pineapple Juice
  • White rum
  • Coconut cream.
  • A blender.

Procedure

Finally getting to the fun part.

  1. Put all the ingredients along with a handful of ice in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. our into a tall glass and garnish as you like.

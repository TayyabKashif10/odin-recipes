Tears of your Enemy

Intro

What better drink than vengeance?.

This innovative drink provides a way to attain refreshment.

While at the same time, getting rid of an enemy.

Isn't that the best?

Ingredients

  • An enemy (preferrably weak)
  • A glass
  • A strong fist.

Procedure

Finally getting to the fun part.

  1. Strap your enemy to a chair.
  2. Put glass underneath enemy's face.
  3. Bring out your fist.
  4. Start bea- tickling the hell out of the enemy's feet.
  5. Tears should start rolling out at this point.
  6. Collect and enjoy

THE GLOP

Intro

Mankind has always been a fan of mixing things together.

This drink is the epitome of this human trait.

Satisfy your inner child and make the best glop ever!

Ingredients

  • Water
  • A glass
  • 10 random edible(optional) things you can find from your home.
  • A blender.

Procedure

Finally getting to the fun part.

  1. Pour water in glass.
  2. Add all the random things.
  3. Mix everything in the blender.
  4. Beautiful.

Dietz Nuts Recipe

Intro

The classic, the OG.

They're not just nuts. They're MEAT nuts.

The introduction of Dietz Nuts brought a paradigm shift in internet culture; now you can learn how to make them yourself!

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Beef
  • Honey
  • Salt
  • Bunch of chemical stuff.

Procedure

Finally getting to the fun part.

  1. Take the beef and pork.
  2. Ground them together.
  3. Shape them into small balls
  4. Add honey and salt.
  5. Add the chemical stuff.
  6. Bake em, pack em. Done.
